ref() reference guide

A case-insensitive Excel-style reference bridge from R formulas to workbook cells.

Single cell

=ref("A1")
=ref("r8c2")

A1 and R1C1 addresses are accepted and are case-insensitive.

Rectangular range

=ref("A1:G5")

A range becomes an R scalar, vector, or matrix as appropriate.

Dynamic spill

=ref("B8#")

The trailing hash reads the complete spill and preserves supported R objects.

Another worksheet

=ref("'Data & Pivot'!A5#")

Quote sheet names containing spaces or punctuation.

Workbook name

=mean(ref("example_numbers"))
